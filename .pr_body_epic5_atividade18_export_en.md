## Summary

- Adds reconciliation report persistence after `POST /reconcile`, including generated `analysis_id` and `expires_at` in the intake response.
- Implements export generation for reconciliation output in both `XLSX` and `CSV` formats.
- Adds new endpoint `GET /reconcile-report/{analysis_id}?format=xlsx|csv` with proper media types and not-found handling.
- Adds frontend download links for reconciliation exports (XLSX/CSV) using returned `analysis_id`.

## Why

EPIC 5 activity 18 requires exportable reconciliation results so users can act on detailed status/match data outside the UI and share outputs operationally.
